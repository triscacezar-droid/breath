The Dhammapada – Selected Verses (Public Domain)

All experience is preceded by mind, led by mind, made by mind.
Speak or act with a corrupted mind, and suffering follows
as the wagon wheel follows the hoof of the ox.

All experience is preceded by mind, led by mind, made by mind.
Speak or act with a peaceful mind, and happiness follows
like a never-departing shadow.

"He abused me, he struck me, he overpowered me, he robbed me."
Those who hold such thoughts will never be free from hatred.

"He abused me, he struck me, he overpowered me, he robbed me."
Those who do not hold such thoughts will be free from hatred.

For hatred is never appeased by hatred in this world.
It is appeased by loving-kindness. This is an eternal law.
